2048 Game

Welcome to the 2048 game! Join the numbers and get to the 2048 tile!

How to Play:

Use your arrow keys to move the tiles. When two tiles with the same number touch, they merge into one!

  • Keyboard: Use Arrow keys (Up, Down, Left, Right)
  • Mouse: Click and drag to move tiles
  • Touch: Swipe to move tiles (for touch devices)
